Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about The Maccabee's Mission!

What is The Maccabee's Mission?

The Maccabee's Mission is a home-based Chanukah treasure hunt designed for the whole family. Follow the clues to find the hidden jug of oil and experience the joy of Chanukah in an interactive and engaging way!

Who is this activity suitable for?

Ages 4-6 can be involved and will definitely enjoy running to help find the next clue once a puzzle or riddle is solved. Ages 7-9 will be able to help solve some of the clues, but it's the 10+ group that will actually do most of the thinking!

How does it work?

The treasure hunt can be set up right in your own home! You'll receive a series of clues that guide participants from one location to the next, culminating in the discovery of the "hidden jug of oil" - a fun nod to the Chanukah miracle.

How long does it take?

The treasure hunt takes approximately an hour, give or take. It's perfect for a Chanukah night activity!

Do I need any special materials?

All you need is the treasure hunt kit and regular household furniture and judaica items where the clues will be hidden. It requires an adult to follow clear directions and set it up. Setup takes around 15 minutes max.

How do I get the treasure hunt kit?

We can ship anywhere in the U.S. for $5, or pickup is available in Crown Heights, Brooklyn.
